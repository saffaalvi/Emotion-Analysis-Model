    Model Summary

    The Sequential CNN model is built with the following configurations:

    • 3 Conv1D layers, each with the ReLU activation function
    • 2 Dropout layers, rate of 20%
    • 1 Flatten layer
    • 3 Dense layers, using the ReLU and Softmax activation functions

    Fitting the Model (Training)

    After training the model, a final accuracy of ~97.85% and validation accuracy of ~92.97% are achieved.

    Observations

    As shown in the confusion matrix, our model was able to predict strong emotions (ie. disgust, anger, calm, happy) with high accuracy. However, the model would often confuse the emotion 'neutral' with 'calm' or 'sad'. It would also confuse 'fear' with 'happy'.

    Since the RAVDESS dataset consisted of audio files where 'calm' and 'neutral' had similar tones and pitch, the model may have had trouble distinguishing the two emotions for this reason. The same concept may apply to the 'fearful' and 'happy' emotions as well.
